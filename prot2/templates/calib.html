Calibration Help

What is Behavioral Calibration?

Behavioral calibration creates a unique profile of how you interact with your computer. This includes your typing rhythm, mouse movement patterns, and other behavioral characteristics.

Tips for Best Results:

  • Be Natural: Type and move your mouse as you normally would
  • Stay Consistent: Use the same hand positions and posture throughout
  • Take Your Time: There's no rush - accuracy is more important than speed
  • Avoid Distractions: Focus on the tasks to ensure consistent data

Privacy & Security:

Your behavioral data is encrypted and stored securely. We only analyze patterns, not the actual content of what you type. This data is used exclusively for authentication purposes.
