Challenge Help

What is a Security Challenge?

Security challenges are used to verify your identity when unusual behavior is detected. They analyze your unique typing and interaction patterns to confirm you are the legitimate user.

Types of Challenges:

üîç Verification Challenge:

Type a short phrase to verify your identity. Used when suspicious activity is detected.

üîÑ Adaptation Challenge:

Type a longer passage to update your behavioral profile when your patterns have naturally evolved.

Tips for Success:

  • Type at your normal, comfortable speed
  • Use your usual hand positions and posture
  • Focus on accuracy rather than speed
  • Avoid distractions and interruptions
  • Don't try to alter your typing style

What happens next?

After completing the challenge, our system will analyze your behavioral patterns and either:

  • Grant access if verification is successful
  • Request retry if results are inconclusive
  • Terminate session if verification fails repeatedly
